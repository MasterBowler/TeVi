Global Terrorism Database

The Global Terrorism Database (GTD) is an open-source database including information on terrorist attacks around the world from 1970 through 2017. The GTD includes systematic data includes more than 180,000 attacks.

Uses of the Dataset

The GTD has been leveraged extensively in scholarly publications, reports, and media articles. Putting Terrorism in Context: Lessons from the Global Terrorism Database, by GTD principal investigators investigates patterns of terrorism and provides perspective on the challenges of data collection and analysis.
